About BMW M4

The BMW M4 Competition is a luxury high-performance sports coupe powered by a 3.0L BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine producing up to 503 horsepower. It combines aggressive styling, premium comfort, advanced technology, and outstanding driving performance.
